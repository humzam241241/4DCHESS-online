Quick Reference

4-King Chess is played on a standard 8×8 board with four armies, one at each corner. Each corner throne square begins with a King and Bishop stacked together. Standard chess piece movement applies. Capture a King to eliminate that player. Last standing wins.

Chaturaji

Quick Reference

Chaturaji ("Four Kings") is an ancient Indian four-player chess variant dating back to the 7th century. Players roll dice to determine which piece type to move. Pieces include the King, Elephant (Rook), Horse (Knight), Boat (Bishop), and Pawn.
